  2. /explore/2 — Colorful + Playful (saturated colors, bold type, fun energy)
  3. /explore/3 — Glassmorphism (frosted glass panels, dark gradient, Apple-like polish)

  5. /explore/5 — Video Game-Inspired (dark HUD, neon accents, typing animations)

  2. Click through each of the 5 exploration links

  4. Decide which direction resonates most (or which 1-2 you want iterated on)
  5. Respond with your choice:
     - "Go with [number]" — locks that direction as the final choice
     - "Iterate on [number] with [feedback]" — triggers refinement before locking
     - "Combine [X] and [Y]" — merge elements from two directions
